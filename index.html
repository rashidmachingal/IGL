Our Services

We Offer Services

We offer a wide array of value-added services to ensure a seamless experience for our clients. From product sourcing to logistical support, we make it easy to import products from China. Here are the services we provide

Product Sourcing

We specialize in sourcing high-quality products from trusted manufacturers in China. With our expertise, we ensure that you get the best deals and products suited to your business needs.

LCL (Less-than-Container Load)

For smaller shipments, we offer LCL services, allowing you to consolidate goods from multiple suppliers into one container, optimizing cost and space.



Own Branding

We offer branding services, allowing you to personalize products with your logo, labels, and packaging for a customized experience.



Door-to-Door Delivery

We ensure door-to-door delivery, handling the entire logistics process from China to your doorstep, saving you time and hassle.


Port Clearance

We manage port clearance smoothly, ensuring your goods are cleared through customs and ready for delivery without delays.


Quality Analysis & Third-Party Quality Checking

We conduct thorough quality analysis and third-party quality checks to ensure all products meet international standards before shipping.

Documentation and Legal Services

We assist with all necessary documentation and legal requirements, ensuring compliance with international trade regulations.

Business Travel Assistance

We offer business travel assistance* to help you plan your visits to suppliers and trade fairs, ensuring a smooth trip.



Logistical Support

From warehousing to shipping coordination, we offer full logistical support to ensure your products are handled with care throughout the shipping process.

Trade Fair Participation, Vendor Screening & Coordination

We help you participate in global trade fairs and manage vendor screening and coordination, making it easier for you to connect with the right suppliers.
